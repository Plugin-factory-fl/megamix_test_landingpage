# MegaMix AI — Product Overview

**Summary · Features · User Benefits · Competitive Position**

---

## 1. Summary

**MegaMix AI** is a web-based AI mixing and mastering platform that turns stems or a rough stereo mix into a finished, release-ready track in minutes. Users upload their tracks, choose a genre and optional guidance, run a one-click AI mix, then refine the result using natural language with **Josh**—an in-app AI assistant—or by tweaking a full per-track mixer. An optional **AI Mastering** step delivers a loud, streaming-ready master with downloadable WAV. No DAW or engineering experience is required; the entire workflow runs in the browser with no install.

---

## 2. Description of Features

### 2.1 Upload & Session Setup
- **Upload stems or a stereo mix** — Drag-and-drop or browse; WAV and MP3 supported.
- **Up to 32 files** — Enough for full multitrack sessions (vocals, drums, bass, guitars, keys, etc.).
- **Clear flow** — Step 1 (Upload), Step 2 (Choose how to mix), Step 3 (Adjust and iterate).

### 2.2 AI Mix (Step 2)
- **Genre presets** — Rock, Metal, Hip-Hop, Pop, EDM, R&B, Jazz, Funk, Country, or Custom.
- **Guidance for the mix** — Quick prompts (e.g. “Clean and present”, “Vocal forward”, “Punchy drums”, “Warm low end”, “Bright and open”, “More glue”) or free-form text (e.g. “punchy drums, vocal forward, warm low end”).
- **One-click “Mix it”** — AI analyzes the session and applies balance, EQ, compression, and tone to match the chosen genre and guidance.

### 2.3 Listen & Refine with Josh (Step 3)
- **Before/After playback** — Compare flat mix (Before) vs. current mix (After) with shared transport, waveform, and seek.
- **Josh, the AI assistant** — Conversational refinement in plain English (e.g. “bring up the vocals”, “more punch”, “brighter”, “warmer”). Josh interprets intent and applies the right mix moves.
- **Floating Josh avatar** — Always-available chat (small input bar or expanded chat panel) so users can ask questions or request changes without leaving the playback view.
- **“What Josh did” transparency** — Optional view of what changes Josh applied.
- **Undo / Redo** — Revert or reapply Josh-driven (and mixer) changes so users can experiment safely.

### 2.4 Manual Mixer
- **Per-track controls** — Level (fader), pan, and optional FX per stem.
- **EQ, compression, reverb** — Per-track EQ, compression, and reverb; changes are reflected in the After playback and when refining with Josh.
- **Live updates** — Mixer adjustments are heard immediately in the transport player.

### 2.5 Export & Mastering
- **Download mix (no mastering)** — Export the current mix as WAV for use in a DAW or other tools.
- **AI Mastering** — One-click mastering with progress feedback (typically 10–30 seconds). Delivers a loud, polished master suitable for streaming.
- **Mastering controls** — Optional real-time tweaks (threshold, ratio, attack, release, output, “Adjust”, dry/wet) for preview; final download uses the rendered master.
- **Refine your master with Josh** — Chat and quick prompts on the mastering view (e.g. “Make it louder”, “More punch”, “Brighter master”, “Warmer”, “More clarity”, “Less compression”).
- **Download WAV** — Final mastered file ready for Spotify, Apple Music, and other platforms.

### 2.6 Licensing & Access
- **Web app** — No install; runs in the browser. Login or free trial for download.
- **Plugin** — MegaMix AI is also available as a DAW plugin (master + per-track instances) with the same Josh persona and natural-language control for in-DAW workflows.

## 4. Competitive Position vs. Incumbents (e.g. Cryo Mix)

### 4.1 Cryo Mix (representative incumbent)
- **Product:** AI-powered online mixing and mastering; credit-based pricing (Essential, Creator, Pro, Pro Annual, custom).
- **Features:** Multi-stem upload (e.g. up to 32 stems in V2), “Nova” conversational assistant, EQ/compression/tonal transparency, Audio Separator, Beat Optimizer, “Magic Touch” reverb, simple and advanced modes, multi-genre.
- **Positioning:** Professional and prosumer; credits often charged on download.

### 4.2 Where MegaMix AI Stands

| Dimension | MegaMix AI | Cryo Mix (incumbent) |
|----------|------------|----------------------|
| **Delivery** | Web app (no install) + optional DAW plugin | Online platform (browser) |
| **Stem capacity** | Up to 32 files (stems or stereo mix) | Up to 32 stems (V2) |
| **AI assistant** | **Josh** — single, consistent persona across web app and plugin; natural-language refinement on mix and master | **Nova** — conversational assistant for mix changes |
| **Workflow** | Linear: Upload → Genre + guidance → Mix it → Refine with Josh / mixer → Export mix → (optional) AI Mastering → Refine master with Josh → Download WAV | Mixing + mastering with simple/advanced modes and extra tools (separator, beat optimizer, reverb) |
| **Transparency** | “What Josh did” + per-track mixer with level, pan, EQ, comp, reverb | Shows what EQ, compression, and tonal shaping were applied per stem |
| **Manual control** | Full per-track mixer (level, pan, EQ, compression, reverb); undo/redo for Josh + mixer | Advanced mode for detailed control |
| **Mastering** | Dedicated AI Mastering step with Josh refinement and optional real-time controls; download rendered WAV | Integrated in platform; credit-based |
| **Plugin** | Same Josh experience in DAW (Logic, Pro Tools, Ableton, FL Studio, etc.) | N/A (web-focused) |
| **Pricing / access** | Login or free trial for download; plugin licensed separately | Credit-based (Essential / Creator / Pro / Pro Annual / custom) |

### 4.3 MegaMix AI Advantages (summary)
- **Unified Josh experience** — One assistant for both web and plugin; same vocabulary and behavior.
- **No install for web** — Instant start in the browser; plugin optional for DAW users.
- **Straightforward flow** — Upload → Mix it → Refine (Josh + mixer) → Optional AI Mastering → Download; easy to explain and use.
- **Undo/Redo** — Safe iteration on both Josh and mixer changes.
- **Always-available Josh** — Floating avatar and chat keep refinement one click away during mix and master.
- **Release-ready in one session** — Mix and master in the same app with consistent language-based control.

### 4.4 Incumbent strengths (for context)
- **Cryo Mix:** Mature credit system, extra tools (separator, beat optimizer, Magic Touch), strong transparency, broad genre support.
- **Others:** Various combinations of stem limits, pricing, and assistant UX.

**Bottom line:** MegaMix AI competes by offering a simple, browser-first path from stems to mastered WAV, with a single AI assistant (Josh) for mix and master refinement, optional full mixer control, and the same assistant in a DAW plugin—positioned for users who want “pro sound in minutes” without learning a complex tool or credit mechanics up front.
